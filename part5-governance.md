# Part V: Governance & Controls

Part V establishes governance frameworks, policies, standards, and compliance requirements for workforce and talent management.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 15: Governance Framework and Controls](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/15-governance/)
Governance structure, workforce governance committee, decision rights, 8 control objectives, risk management, and audit requirements.

### [Chapter 16: Policies, Standards, and Compliance](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/16-policies-compliance/)
Policy framework, key workforce policies, employment law compliance (EEOC, ADA, FMLA, GDPR), labor relations, and audit readiness.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part V, you will be able to:

- Establish effective workforce governance structures
- Implement control objectives for workforce management
- Ensure compliance with employment laws and regulations
- Prepare for workforce-related audits
