# Part III: Technical Implementation

Part III covers the technical aspects of workforce and talent management. These chapters provide guidance on HR technology, learning systems, performance management, and integration with ITSM practices.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 8: HR Technology and Systems](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/08-hr-technology/)
HRIS/HCM platforms, workforce management tools, applicant tracking systems, integration architecture, and system selection.

### [Chapter 9: Learning Management and Development](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/09-learning-management/)
LMS platforms, training delivery methods, certification programs, knowledge management, and career development.

### [Chapter 10: Performance Management Systems](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/10-performance-management/)
Goal setting (OKRs, SMART), continuous feedback, performance reviews, 360-degree feedback, and recognition programs.

### [Chapter 11: Integration with ITSM Practices](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/11-itsm-integration/)
Integration with Incident, Problem, Change, Service Desk, and Knowledge Management practices.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part III, you will be able to:

- Select and implement appropriate HR technology solutions
- Design effective learning and development programs
- Establish comprehensive performance management systems
- Integrate workforce management with ITSM practices
