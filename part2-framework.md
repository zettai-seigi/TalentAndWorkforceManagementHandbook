# Part II: Framework

Part II covers the operational framework for workforce and talent management. These chapters provide comprehensive guidance on workforce planning, talent acquisition, competency development, and operational workflows.

---

## Chapters in This Part

### [Chapter 4: Workforce Planning and Capacity Management](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/04-workforce-planning/)
Strategic workforce planning, demand forecasting, capacity analysis, gap analysis, and workforce modeling.

### [Chapter 5: Talent Acquisition and Recruitment](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/05-talent-acquisition/)
Recruitment strategy, sourcing channels, employer branding, selection processes, and onboarding programs.

### [Chapter 6: Roles, Responsibilities, and Competency Frameworks](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/06-competency-frameworks/)
ITSM role definitions, RACI matrices, competency models, skill taxonomies, and career paths.

### [Chapter 7: Workflows and Processes](/TalentAndWorkforceManagementHandbook/chapters/07-workflows/)
End-to-end workforce processes including hiring, performance review, training, resource allocation, and offboarding workflows.

---

## Learning Outcomes

After completing Part II, you will be able to:

- Develop strategic workforce plans aligned with business objectives
- Design effective recruitment and talent acquisition strategies
- Create comprehensive competency frameworks and career paths
- Implement standardized workforce management workflows
