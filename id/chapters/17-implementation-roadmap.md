# Bab 17: Peta Jalan Implementasi

## Tujuan Pembelajaran

- Merencanakan implementasi manajemen tenaga kerja
- Mengidentifikasi persyaratan sumber daya
- Mengelola stakeholder
- Mendefinisikan kriteria keberhasilan

---

## Fase 1: Foundation

### Aktivitas Utama

| Aktivitas | Output | Timeline |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Maturity assessment | Assessment report | Minggu 1-4 |
| Stakeholder analysis | Stakeholder map | Minggu 2-3 |
| Vision & strategy | Strategy document | Minggu 4-8 |
| Quick wins | Implemented improvements | Minggu 8-24 |
| Governance setup | Committee formed | Minggu 4-8 |

### Quick Wins

1. Standarisasi job descriptions
2. Implementasi onboarding checklist
3. Launch employee engagement survey
4. Setup basic workforce reporting

## Kriteria Keberhasilan

### KPI per Fase

| Fase | KPI Target |
|------|------------|
| **Foundation** | Governance established, assessment complete |
| **Build** | Systems live, 80% process documented |
| **Scale** | 100% coverage, analytics operational |
| **Optimize** | KPI targets met, Level 4+ maturity |

---

## Ringkasan

Implementasi yang sukses membutuhkan:
- Pendekatan bertahap yang realistis
- Quick wins untuk membangun momentum
- Stakeholder engagement yang kuat
- Pengukuran progress yang konsisten
