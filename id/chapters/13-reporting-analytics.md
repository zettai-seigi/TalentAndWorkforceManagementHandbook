# Bab 13: Pelaporan dan Analitik

## Tujuan Pembelajaran

- Merancang sistem pelaporan tenaga kerja
- Mengimplementasikan workforce analytics
- Membuat dashboard yang efektif
- Menerapkan analitik prediktif

---

## Kerangka Analitik Tenaga Kerja

### Tingkat Analitik

| Tingkat | Fokus | Pertanyaan | Contoh |
|---------|-------|------------|--------|
| **Descriptive** | Apa yang terjadi? | Past analysis | Turnover rate |
| **Diagnostic** | Mengapa terjadi? | Root cause | Exit interview analysis |
| **Predictive** | Apa yang akan terjadi? | Forecasting | Attrition prediction |
| **Prescriptive** | Apa yang harus dilakukan? | Recommendations | Retention strategies |

---

## Laporan Standar

### Laporan Bulanan

| Laporan | Audiens | Konten |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Headcount Report** | HR, Finance | HC, FTE, movements |
| **Recruitment Report** | HR, Hiring Mgrs | Pipeline, hires, metrics |
| **Training Report** | HR, L&D | Completions, hours |
| **Performance Report** | HR, Managers | Ratings, distributions |

### Laporan Kuartalan

| Laporan | Audiens | Konten |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Workforce Review** | Leadership | Comprehensive overview |
| **Talent Review** | HR, Managers | Succession, development |
| **Engagement Report** | Leadership | Survey results |

---

## Dashboard Design

### Prinsip Dashboard yang Baik

1. **Fokus** - Satu tujuan per dashboard
2. **Clarity** - Visualisasi yang jelas
3. **Action-oriented** - Insight yang actionable
4. **Real-time** - Data terkini
5. **Interactive** - Drill-down capability

---

## Analitik Prediktif

### Use Cases

| Use Case | Data Input | Output |
|----------|------------|--------|
| **Attrition Risk** | Performance, engagement, tenure | Risk score |
| **Hiring Success** | Interview scores, background | Success probability |
| **Skill Gap** | Current vs future needs | Gap forecast |

---

## Ringkasan

Analitik tenaga kerja yang efektif:
- Dimulai dari descriptive, berkembang ke predictive
- Menggunakan visualisasi yang tepat
- Memberikan insight yang actionable
- Mendukung pengambilan keputusan data-driven
